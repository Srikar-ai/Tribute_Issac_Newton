Issac Newton

The man who discovered gravity

About Him Isaac Newton (born January 4 1643 ) Woolsthorpe-by-Colsterworth, Lincolnshire, England and died on 31 March 1727 Kensington, Middlesex, Great Britain.

Education

His Education was in Trinity College,Cambridge.

Discoveries

1)In optics, his discovery of the composition of white light integrated the phenomena of colours into the science of light and laid the foundation for modern physical optics.

2) In mechanics, his three laws of motion, the basic principles of modern physics, resulted in the formulation of the law of universal gravitation.

3)In mathematics, he was the original discoverer of the infinitesimal calculus.
